Nails Polish & Pamper

Our Nails Polish & Pamper services offer a luxurious experience to enhance the beauty and health of your nails. From basic manicures to intricate nail art, our skilled technicians use premium products to deliver stunning, long-lasting results tailored to your style and preferences.

Details:

  • Service Type: Manicures, pedicures, gel polish, BIAB treatments, and nail art for hands and feet.
  • Techniques: Precision nail shaping, cuticle care, polish application, and specialized treatments like BIAB and callus peels.
  • Products: High-quality gel, BIAB, and normal polishes, plus nourishing treatments for healthy nails.
  • Location: Conveniently located in the heart of the city with easy access and ample parking.
  • Service Duration: Sessions range from 5 to 110 minutes depending on the service.
  • Cost: Affordable pricing starting at £1.99, with luxury packages available.

Service Highlights:

Customized Care: Each session is tailored to your nail goals, ensuring a personalized pampering experience.
Aftercare Tips: Technicians provide advice on maintaining your manicure or pedicure, including product recommendations and care routines.

More Details

Versatile Options: From classic polish to advanced BIAB treatments and creative nail art, we cater to all nail care needs.

Expert Precision: Our technicians are trained in the latest nail techniques to ensure flawless application and lasting results.

Benefits to the Client:

Enhanced Appearance: Beautifully polished nails elevate your look and boost confidence.
Long-Lasting Results: High-quality products and techniques ensure durable, chip-free finishes.

Relaxation: Pampering services provide a soothing escape, leaving you refreshed and rejuvenated.
