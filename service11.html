Hair & Styling

Hair & Styling services are designed to enhance your natural beauty and express your unique style. Our expert stylists use advanced techniques and premium products to deliver personalized haircuts, styling, and coloring that suit your preferences and lifestyle, ensuring a polished look for every occasion.

Details:

  • Service Type: Comprehensive haircuts, styling, and coloring for all hair types and lengths.
  • Techniques: Precision cutting, blow-drying, curling, straightening, and color application for grey coverage.
  • Products: High-quality, salon-grade products to ensure vibrant, healthy, and manageable hair.
  • Location: Conveniently located in the heart of the city with easy access and ample parking.
  • Service Duration: Sessions range from 20 to 60 minutes depending on the service.
  • Cost: Affordable pricing starting at £14.99, with premium options for specialized treatments.

Service Highlights:

Personalized Consultations: Each client receives a tailored consultation to discuss hair goals, ensuring results that exceed expectations.
Maintenance Advice: Our stylists provide expert tips on maintaining your hairstyle at home, including product and care recommendations.

More Details

Versatile Services: From classic trims to bold restyles and vibrant color applications, we cater to all hair styling needs.

Expert Craftsmanship: Our stylists are trained in the latest techniques to deliver flawless cuts, styles, and treatments with precision.

Benefits to the Client:

Boosted Confidence: A fresh haircut or vibrant style enhances your appearance and self-esteem.
Professional Results: Access to skilled stylists and top-tier products ensures long-lasting, high-quality outcomes.

Time-Saving: Efficient services deliver stunning results in minimal time, fitting seamlessly into your schedule.
