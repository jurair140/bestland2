Child Treatments

Our Child Treatments are specially designed for young clients, offering gentle and age-appropriate beauty services to make them feel pampered and confident. From brow grooming to manicures and lash enhancements, our skilled technicians use safe, high-quality products to ensure a fun and comfortable experience.

Details:

  • Service Type: Brow threading, waxing, manicures, pedicures, lash treatments, and acrylic extensions tailored for children and teens aged 3-16 years.
  • Techniques: Gentle grooming methods suited for young skin and nails, ensuring safety and comfort.
  • Products: Child-safe, high-quality polishes, waxes, and lash products for a gentle pampering experience.
  • Location: Conveniently located in the heart of the city with easy access and ample parking.
  • Service Duration: Quick sessions ranging from 5 to 60 minutes, perfect for young clients.
  • Cost: Affordable pricing starting at £3.99, with fun packages like the Princess Package available.

Service Highlights:

Age-Appropriate Care: Each service is customized to suit the age and comfort level of the child, ensuring a positive experience.
Aftercare Guidance: Technicians provide simple tips for parents and kids to maintain their treatments at home.

More Details

Fun and Safe Services: From playful nail treatments to gentle lash enhancements, our services are designed to delight young clients.

Expert Care: Our technicians are trained to work with children, ensuring a safe, friendly, and professional environment.

Benefits to the Client:

Boosted Confidence: Fun beauty treatments help young clients feel special and confident.
Safe and Gentle: Child-friendly products and techniques ensure comfort and safety.

Quick and Enjoyable: Short sessions keep kids engaged while delivering beautiful results.
